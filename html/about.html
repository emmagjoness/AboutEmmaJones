About Me

this is who I am.

My name is Emma Jones. I was born in Tulsa, Oklahoma and lived there my whole life until August of 2021. This past fall, I moved to Provo, Utah to begin my undergraduate education at Brigham Young University.

I love it here at BYU! I have made so many wonderful friends and memories over the last 8 months. I am a Pre-Business major and have spent a lot of time studying this year (gotta keep the GPA up)! I think Brigham Young University's campus is beautiful and I am forever grateful for the opportunity I have to attend this university.

After I complete my undergraduate studies, I plan to pursue a law degree. In the interest of being frank, I have no idea where I want to go to law school. In a dream world, I would move back to somewhere in the south and work as counsel for a corporation as an attorney practicing corporate law.
